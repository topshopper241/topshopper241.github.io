Autosync for Google Drive - efficiency - productivity

Full automatic two-way synchronization of files and folders

This app is a tool for synchronizing and backing up files. It allows you to automatically sync files and folders with Google Drive cloud storage and your other devices. The app is an ideal tool for synchronizing photos, documents, for automatic data transfer, the automatic sharing of files on different devices, ...

New files from your cloud account are automatically downloaded to your device. New files on your device will be uploaded. If you delete a file on one side, it will also be deleted on the other. This app works on various devices (smartphone, tablet). If their folders are connected to the same cloud account, they are synchronized with each other.

This is how Google Drive works on computers, but not on Android. Automatic two-way syncing should be an essential feature of the official app. For whatever reason, it is not. Autosync for Box is there to fill this gap.

No external users can decrypt, view or change file contents

All data transfers and communication between the user's devices and the servers of the cloud storage are securely encrypted and do not run through our servers
